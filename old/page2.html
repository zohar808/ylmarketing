About YL Marketing

Founded in 2005 by Mr. Yehuda Levin, YL Marketing specializes in audio\video systems and strives to expand the trading and distribution of such products within the broadcasting and telecommunications industries.

Before starting his own business, Mr. Levin acquired 20 years of experience working in the television and video communications field. He held topnotch sales positions in leading companies such as: Orad®, Optibase®, Scopus®, and Foxcom®.

Through his work, Mr. Yehuda Levin traveled on a regular basis and used his networking skills to establish excellent business relations. He created a network of prominent distributors and key account customers, and developed reputable relations with government offices.

Mr. Zohar Levin, who is Yehuda Levin's son, is in charge of executing operation procedures in YL Marketing. Outside of the company, he also engages in marketing and is currently studying in Tel Aviv University as a double major in Journalism and Communication.

Our services

YL Marketing works with both ends of the spectrum- providing consulting and sales promotion services to companies who sell audio and video professional  equipment, as well as to broadcasting and corporate networks who seek for high quality audio and video communications professional solutions.

In addition to that, YL Marketing can train your company's regional sales managers and assist them with developing and expanding the territory they are covering.

YL Marketing is currently covering Eastern Europe and the Balkans. During the past six years, YL Marketing executive Yehuda Levin installed many ORAD systems (On Air Graphics and Virtual Studios, as well as Sport systems- including automation systems that are MOS based) in these countries.

YL Marketing is also ready and willing to promote your company's audio and video professional solutions in additional European countries, upon your individual request and vision for your business.

As a diverse company, YL Marketing offers the full package of its services, vast knowledge and experience in the field, as well as great flexibility at surprisingly reasonable, competitive rates. This way, you may save a lot of money, boost your turn-over, and increase your market share.

Throughout the past year, YL Marketing has consulted to companies such as Kramer®, WASP3D®, and Actus®.

Our objectives

1. Cost efficiency- dramatically lowering the costs of hiring sales personal for your company, as well as balancing your company’s manpower strength in order to spare your business from spending precious time and extra funds that could be used for more important administration resources.

2. Time to market- using our knowledge of the field as well as our established network of customers and distributors, which ensures a quick, effective integration of your company’s products into the targeted market.

3. Increasing market share- guaranteed success, which guarantees a higher amount of sales and an increasing market share for your company.
